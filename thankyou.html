COLOUR GRADING GUIDE

How to Apply a LUT in Final Cut Pro

A seven-step walkthrough for loading your FOCL Media LUTs and creating a clean, cinematic grade.

Step 1

Select the clip (or adjustment layer) you want to colour grade.

Step 2

Open the Effects Browser (bottom right of the screen) and search for Custom LUT.

Step 3

Drag and drop Custom LUT onto your selected clip or adjustment layer.

Step 4

Open the Inspector (top right), then under Custom LUT click Choose Custom LUT.

Step 5

Locate your downloaded LUT folder and import it.

Step 6

Your LUTs will now appear inside the Custom LUT dropdown menu. Choose the LUT you would like to use.

Step 7

Adjust exposure, contrast and white balance to achieve the best possible cinematic look.
